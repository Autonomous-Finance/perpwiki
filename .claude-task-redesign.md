# Task: HyperWiki Visual Redesign — Less Neon, More Intelligence

## Problem
The current design looks like a Hyperliquid brand extension:
- `#00E5A0` is Hyperliquid's exact brand green — we're a third-party directory, not their marketing
- Scanlines, neon glow pulses, heavy green shadows = "vibe-coded" hackathon aesthetic
- We need to look like an **independent intelligence resource** — distinct, credible, not a clone

## Direction
**From**: Neon trading terminal (Hyperliquid's own aesthetic)  
**To**: Financial intelligence dashboard — darker, more editorial, more authoritative

Reference feel: Delphi Digital, Messari, L2Beat — professional crypto research tools that feel trustworthy.

## Remove / Tone Down Effects

### Remove scanlines entirely
In `globals.css`, delete the `.scanlines::after` block. Remove `scanlines` class from `src/app/page.tsx` hero section.

### Replace green glow with subtle blue glow
Find all `rgba(0, 229, 160, ...)` in globals.css and components. Replace with `rgba(75, 142, 244, ...)` at HALF the opacity. Example:
- `rgba(0, 229, 160, 0.06)` → `rgba(75, 142, 244, 0.04)`
- `rgba(0, 229, 160, 0.3)` → `rgba(75, 142, 244, 0.15)` 

### ProjectCard hover
Currently: `hover:border-[var(--hl-green)] hover:shadow-[0_0_12px_rgba(0,229,160,0.3)]`  
Change to: `hover:border-[var(--hw-blue)] hover:shadow-[0_0_8px_rgba(75,142,244,0.15)]`  
Much more subtle. Less glow = more professional.

### Hero radial gradient
Currently a strong green radial glow at top. Replace:
`rgba(0,229,160,0.06)` → `rgba(75,142,244,0.04)` (barely visible blue tint, almost gone)
